Our Featured Accommodations

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts.

Resorts: Taniti's four-star luxary resort define opulence with their world-class amenities, spas and fine dining options. It is the ultimate treat for your luxorius stay in Taniti.

Guest Houses: the budget-conscious traveler seeking an authentic tanitian experience, we also feature guesthouses that offer comfortable accommodations and a chance to connect with local hosts.

Villas & Vacation Rentals: If you desire privacy and flexibility, our listings of private villas and vacation rentals offer the perfect solution.

*All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
